Joint Pain

Joint pain is a common condition affecting daily movement and quality of life. In Ayurveda, joint pain is primarily linked to Vata imbalance, toxin accumulation (Ama), poor digestion, aging, and lifestyle factors.

Diabetes

Diabetes, known as Madhumeha in Ayurveda, is a chronic metabolic condition caused by impaired digestion, lifestyle imbalance, stress, and reduced metabolic efficiency. Ayurveda focuses on managing blood sugar levels naturally, improving metabolism, and preventing long-term complications through a holistic approach.

Sexual health

Sexual health is an important part of overall physical and mental well-being. In Ayurveda, sexual health concerns are often linked to stress, lifestyle imbalance, poor digestion, hormonal disturbances, and mental fatigue.

Anorectal Disease

Anorectal diseases affect the anus and rectum, often causing pain, bleeding, discomfort, and difficulty during bowel movements. In Ayurveda, these conditions are mainly linked to poor digestion, chronic constipation, unhealthy diet, and lifestyle imbalance.

Skin disorder like psoriasis with holistic health

Psoriasis and other chronic skin disorders are not just skin-deep conditions. In Ayurveda, such disorders are understood as the result of internal imbalance, poor digestion, toxin accumulation, stress, and immune disturbance.

Stress & Anxiety Management

Stress and anxiety are common in today’s fast-paced lifestyle and can affect mental peace, sleep, digestion, immunity, and overall health. In Ayurveda, stress-related conditions are linked to Vata imbalance, mental overload, poor daily routine, and weakened nervous system.
